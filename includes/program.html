Program

The programme will run from 10.00-18.00 Irish time (GMT+1h) for three days August 22nd-24th, though with the afternoon session on the 24th being the SummDial programme. Breaks and lunchtime will be scheduled within these times. Registration desks will be open from 9.30 each day.
