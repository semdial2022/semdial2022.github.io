The below papers will be presented as poster presentations.INSTRUCTIONS FOR POSTER PRESENTERS: Physical posters should be no wider than 91 cm (36 in) and no higher than 122 cm (48 in), in vertical/portrait orientation. A0 paper (in vertical/portrait orientation) meets these requirements. Please try to bear in mind accessibility and inclusivity for readers when designing your posters, in line with the NAACL poster guidance. If you require local poster printing in Dublin, we recommend Reads Nassau Street and Dublin City University Campus Printing.

For remote poster presenters, please have two slides ready to present for visitors to your Teams meeting, the link for which will be made available via email from the organisers.
