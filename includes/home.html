SemDial 2022 - DubDial

THE

26th WORKSHOP ON THE SEMANTICS AND PRAGMATICS OF DIALOGUE

22-24 September, Technological University Dublin
Ireland

DubDial will be the 26th edition of the SemDial workshop series which aims to bring together researchers working on the semantics and pragmatics of dialogue in fields such as formal semantics and pragmatics, computational linguistics, artificial intelligence, philosophy, psychology, and neuroscience. This year there is a special theme of Interactivism, for which, on top of the more traditional papers on dialogue, we welcome papers on. In 2022 the workshop will be hosted by the Technological University Dublin.
