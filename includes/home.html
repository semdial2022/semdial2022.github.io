SemDial 2022 - DubDial

THE

22-24 August, Technological University Dublin
Ireland // Online (hybrid)

DubDial will be the 26th edition of the SemDial workshop series which aims to bring together researchers working on the semantics and pragmatics of dialogue in fields such as formal semantics and pragmatics, computational linguistics, artificial intelligence, philosophy, psychology, and neuroscience. This year there is a special theme of Interactivism, for which, on top of the more traditional papers on dialogue, we welcome papers on.

In 2022 the workshop will be hosted by the Technological University Dublin and it will be a hybrid meeting where attendance virtually at a reduced fee is possible.

The final session of SemDial will be the second edition of SummDial a special session on Summarization of Dialogues and Multi-Party Meetings, for which there is a separate submission process - please see the SummDial website here.
